### 2. Products covered by this policy

This policy is intended for one-time purchases of self-paced digital
educational products, such as courses, guides, recordings, templates and
downloadable materials.

Before purchase, the relevant product page and checkout will identify the
main product features, included materials, price, currency, applicable taxes

Subscriptions, live programmes, coaching, events, physical goods or payment
plans may require additional terms and must not be sold under this policy
alone unless they are expressly added to it.

### 3. Orders and payment

You will have an opportunity to review and correct your order before making
payment. By completing a purchase, you confirm that the order information is
accurate, that you are legally able to enter into the purchase and that you
are authorised to use the selected payment method.

Prices and accepted payment methods are shown at checkout. Payment is handled
by **[INSERT PAYMENT PROCESSOR]** under that provider's terms and privacy
practices. Dwayne M Cyrus does not store complete payment-card details.

An order is accepted when you receive an electronic order confirmation. If an
order cannot be accepted because of a genuine pricing error, suspected fraud,
payment failure or legal restriction, the payment will be cancelled or
refunded.

### 4. Digital delivery

Unless the product page states a future release date or a different delivery
schedule, access instructions will be sent electronically to the email
address used for purchase after payment is confirmed.

The order confirmation will explain how to access the product. You are
responsible for providing an accurate email address, checking filtered or

[`hey@dwaynecyrus.com`](mailto:hey@dwaynecyrus.com) with the purchaser's name,

The product page will state how long access is provided. “Lifetime access”
will not be promised unless its exact meaning and service limitations are
defined at the time of sale.

Your purchase provides a personal, limited, non-exclusive and
non-transferable licence to use the materials for your own learning. Unless
express written permission is given, you may not share login credentials,
copy or distribute substantial parts of the materials, resell them, publish

### 6. Voluntary 14-day refund promise

**[OWNER APPROVAL REQUIRED]** The recommended customer-friendly position is:

You may request a refund for a one-time self-paced digital product within 14
calendar days after purchase. Send the request to
[`hey@dwaynecyrus.com`](mailto:hey@dwaynecyrus.com) from the purchase email and
include the order number. You do not need to give a reason, although feedback
is welcome.

An approved refund will be issued to the original payment method. It will be
initiated within five business days after approval; the payment provider or
bank may take additional time to show it in your account. Product access may
end when the refund is issued.

The voluntary refund promise applies once per customer for each product and
does not cover duplicate refund requests or misuse of the refund process. None
of these limits reduces any mandatory right you have under applicable law.

### 7. Statutory cancellation and consumer rights

Consumer rights vary by location. Swiss law does not generally provide a
cooling-off period for ordinary online purchases, but this does not remove
rights relating to products that are defective or do not match what was
promised.

Consumers protected by European Union or other local consumer law may have
additional mandatory rights. Where immediate access to digital content is
offered and the law requires it, checkout must ask for the consumer's express
request to begin delivery during a withdrawal period and their acknowledgement
of any resulting loss of a statutory withdrawal right. The voluntary refund
promise above remains available according to its terms.

Nothing in this policy excludes or limits a right that cannot lawfully be
excluded or limited.

### 8. Faulty, unavailable or misdescribed products

If a product cannot be accessed, is materially defective or does not match its
description, contact
[`hey@dwaynecyrus.com`](mailto:hey@dwaynecyrus.com). A reasonable attempt will
be made to restore access, correct the problem or provide replacement
materials. If that cannot be done within a reasonable time, an appropriate
price reduction or refund will be offered as required by applicable law.

### 9. Educational nature and results

The products provide educational and informational material. They do not
provide medical, psychological, legal or financial advice, and they do not
create a therapist-client, doctor-patient or professional advisory
relationship. Individual results depend on many factors and are not
guaranteed.

### 10. Changes to this policy

The policy may be updated for future purchases when products, delivery methods
or legal requirements change. The policy in force when an order was accepted
will continue to govern that order unless a later change is required by law or
is more favourable to the customer.

### 11. Questions and complaints

Questions, delivery problems, cancellation notices and refund requests can be
sent to [`hey@dwaynecyrus.com`](mailto:hey@dwaynecyrus.com).

## Internal drafting notes

- The attached Justin Welsh example was used only to identify topics. Its U.S.
  entity, named products, sponsorship rules, U.S.-dollar clause, card-storage
  statement and payment-security specifications were not copied because they
  are not verified facts about this business.
- Swiss federal guidance says an online seller must clearly identify itself,
  explain the ordering steps, let customers correct errors and promptly
  confirm orders electronically. It also says Switzerland has no general
  statutory withdrawal period for e-commerce, while warranty rights still
  apply.
- EU guidance requires additional pre-contract information when a trader
  targets EU consumers. For immediately supplied digital content, loss of the
  withdrawal right depends on advance express consent and acknowledgement;
  the confirmation must record this.
